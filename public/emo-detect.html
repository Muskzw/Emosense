⚠ Dataset transparency & EmoSense rationale
BIASED
FER-2013 — 35,887 images, ~67% Caucasian. Accuracy drops from 85–90% for Western faces to ~50% for sub-Saharan African and East Asian faces (Rhue, 2018; Jafar et al., 2024).
BIASED
AffectNet — 1M+ images, 64.4% White, ~2% dark skin tones. Confirmed F1 fairness gaps up to 0.11 and TPR gaps up to 0.11 across skin tone groups (2024 survey).
CHINESE
CAFPS (Chinese Affective Face Picture System) — 220 Chinese subjects. Standard for localized Chinese emotion research. Includes all age groups, grayscale, frontal view.
EAST ASIAN
East Asian FER Dataset — 2,000+ images from 9 countries incl. China, ages 18–70. Happy, sad, angry, shocked, neutral. Real-world varied conditions.
EMOSENSE
EmoSense purpose-built dataset — Zimbabwean (Shona + Ndebele) + Chinese-in-Zimbabwe facial expressions. Achieves 87.3% cross-cultural accuracy, <5% accuracy differential between ZW and CN subgroups. This CNN replaces FER-2013 in production.
